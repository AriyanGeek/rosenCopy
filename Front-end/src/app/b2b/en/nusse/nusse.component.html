Pistachios

Pistachios are among the most delicious and valuable nuts. We source our pistachios directly from selected and recognised producers in the sunny region of Iran 'Kerman', where the highest quality pistachios in the world have been grown for centuries.

The Persian pistachios are of outstanding excellent quality due to the optimal climatic conditions in the growing area, which is brushed by the warm winds of the hottest desert in the world, the Lut desert.

The main varieties of Persian pistachios are super long, long, jumbo and round, which differ mainly in their sizes.
The Persian pistachios are a gift from the nature to us.
We proudly guarantee the excellent quality of our pistachios.
