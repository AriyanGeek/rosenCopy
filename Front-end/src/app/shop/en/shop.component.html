Quality promise:

All pistachios of Rosenfarbe are exclusively super long. This type offers the optimal distribution of the ingredients during the roasting process and ensures that the shell is particularly easy to open before consumption.

All nuts and ingredients are selected personally by us. Thanks to our high quality standards, every single product that leaves his home roastery deserves the label 'Hand Selected'.
