About Rosenfarbe

The company Rosenfarbe exists since 2020. The founder, Kambiz Arasteh, worked as a management consultant for companies in the spice, pistachio and nut production industries in Georgia and Iran after studying Industrial Engineering in France and the United Kingdom. Kambiz, himself a great nuts lover, was able to develop unique knowledge and is well networked in this industry. The idea to bring the best products of his network to Europe and to trade with them was born and Kambiz started importing saffron and pistachios.

As a connoisseur with the highest quality standards, Kambiz experimented with his own compositions of the imported products. In his creations he combines the handpicked ingredients to create unique taste experiences. That is why all products carry the ‘Hand Selected’ label.

All ingredients are imported directly from the producers without intermediaries in order to guarantee customers the highest quality and optimal freshness of all products.

Rosenfarbe meaning Rose’s colour in German is one name with many impressions. All over the world, the rose is considered a symbol of beauty and love, which also has something erotic about it. Colours affect people's moods and create lasting impressions. Kambiz was inspired by this when he chose the brand name for his products: From the beauty of every single ingredient, which he combines with a lot of love for something unique and enjoying food, which is always accompanied by a touch of eroticism. The different colours stand for the colourful variety of feelings, experiences and impressions.
